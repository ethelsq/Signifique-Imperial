# Squad Fusion

Fusiona dois ou mais squads em um squad unificado.

---

## Execucao

1. Leia o agente: `squads/squad-creator/agents/squad-chief.md`
2. Adote a persona do Squad Architect
3. Leia a task: `squads/squad-creator/tasks/squad-fusion.md`
4. Execute o workflow: `squads/squad-creator/workflows/wf-squad-fusion.yaml`
5. Resolva conflitos entre agentes, tasks e workflows dos squads fonte
